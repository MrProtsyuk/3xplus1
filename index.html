3X + 1

What is it?

The Collatz Conjecture, or better known as the 3x + 1 problem, is a math problem that has baffled mathematicians for over 50 years. It's easy to explain: take any positive number, if it's even, divide it by 2; if it's odd, multiply it by 3 and add 1. Repeat this process with the resulting number, and the conjecture says that you will eventually reach 1.
(Gomez, Salvador Bermudez. "The 3x + 1 Conjecture, a Direct Path." Scrip.org, 27/June/2023, https://www.scirp.org/journal/paperinformation?paperid=125818#:~:text=The%203x%20%2B%201%20problem%2C%20is,you%20will%20eventually%20reach%201.)
